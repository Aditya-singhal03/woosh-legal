# Woosh AI Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 19, 2026
This Privacy Policy explains what personal data Woosh AI collects, how we use it, and the choices you have.
Thank you for using Woosh AI ("Woosh AI," "we," "us," or "our"). Protecting your privacy is central to how we design and operate our AI-powered photo and video generation tools. This Policy applies to the Woosh AI iOS application and related services (the "Service").
If you do not agree with this Policy, please discontinue use of the Service. Capitalized terms not defined here have the meanings given in the Woosh AI Terms of Service.
## 1. Information We Collect
### 1.1 Account and Contact Information
When you create or manage an account, we collect:
Name, username, and profile information you provide
Email address and authentication identifiers from Apple or Google when you use "Sign in with Apple" or "Sign in with Google"
Billing and purchase information (Apple processes your payment; we receive transaction identifiers and subscription status, not your full payment details)
### 1.2 Creative Inputs and Outputs
To operate the Service, we process and store:
Text prompts you enter
Reference photos, videos, or other files you upload
AI-generated Outputs we produce for you
Edit history and project data
This content is stored on our backend servers so you can access your creations across sessions, view history, and continue editing.
### 1.3 Device and Usage Information
We automatically collect technical data when you use the Service:
Device model, operating system version, app version, and device language
IP address and approximate location derived from IP (country/region level)
Diagnostics such as crash logs, performance data, and interaction events (e.g., features used, generation counts)
### 1.4 Analytics
We use analytics SDKs to understand how users interact with the Service. These SDKs collect usage events and device identifiers. We do not use the App Tracking Transparency framework to track you across other apps and websites for advertising purposes. We do not share your data with third-party advertisers.
### 1.5 Support and Communications
When you contact support@cravix.ai or submit in-app feedback or reports, we collect the information you provide (including attachments) to respond to you and improve the Service.
## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use collected information to:
Provide, personalize, and maintain the Service
Generate and deliver AI-created photos and videos based on your Inputs
Process subscriptions and credit purchases through Apple
Communicate with you about updates, security, and support
Monitor usage, prevent abuse, detect fraud, and enforce our Terms
Develop new features and improve performance using aggregated or de-identified data
Comply with legal obligations and respond to lawful requests
Model training: We do not use your Inputs or Outputs to train our AI models without your explicit, separate consent. If this changes, we will notify you and obtain consent where required by law.
## 3. Legal Basis for Processing (GDPR / UK / EEA / Switzerland)
Where GDPR or similar laws apply, we process personal data based on:
Performance of a contract: providing the Service you requested
Legitimate interests: maintaining and improving the Service, securing infrastructure, preventing abuse
Consent: optional analytics where required by law, marketing communications (where applicable)
Legal obligations: responding to lawful requests, tax and record-keeping requirements
## 4. Sharing Your Information
We do not sell your personal data. We do not share your data for cross-context behavioral advertising.
We share information only with:
Service providers who help operate the Service (cloud hosting, AI model providers, analytics, customer support, payment processing). These providers access data only to perform services on our behalf and are contractually obligated to protect it.
AI model providers to process your Inputs and generate Outputs. Inputs may be sent to third-party AI infrastructure providers for processing. We work with providers that offer appropriate data protection commitments.
Legal authorities when we believe disclosure is required to comply with law, protect our rights or the safety of others, prevent fraud or abuse, or respond to lawful requests.
Business transfers in the event of a merger, acquisition, or asset sale. We will notify you before your personal data is transferred and becomes subject to a different privacy policy.
## 5. Data Retention
We retain personal data for as long as needed to provide the Service, comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements.
Account data: retained while your account is active
Inputs and Outputs: retained while your account is active so you can access your creations
Diagnostic data: typically retained for up to 12 months
Billing records: retained as required by tax and accounting laws (typically 7 years)
When data is no longer required, we delete or anonymize it.
## 6. Account Deletion
You can delete your account directly from within the app at Settings > Account > Delete Account. Upon deletion, we will:
Delete your profile, Inputs, and Outputs within 30 days, except where retention is required by law
Anonymize any aggregated usage data we retain
Cancel any ongoing subscription processing on our side (you must separately cancel your Apple subscription in your iPhone Settings)
You can also request deletion by emailing support@cravix.ai.
## 7. Data Security
We use technical and organizational safeguards to protect personal information against unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration, including encryption in transit, access controls, and monitoring.
No online service is completely secure. Please use strong passwords, keep your device updated, and notify us immediately at support@cravix.ai if you suspect your account has been compromised.
## 8. International Data Transfers
We operate from India and may process data using servers or service providers located in other countries, including the United States and the European Union. When transferring personal data internationally, we rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses or equivalent mechanisms to protect your rights.
## 9. Your Rights and Choices
Depending on where you live, you may have the right to:
Access personal data we hold about you
Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
Delete your personal data
Object to or restrict certain processing activities
Portability — receive a copy of your data in a machine-readable format
Withdraw consent where consent is the legal basis
To exercise these rights, email support@cravix.ai. We may need to verify your identity before responding. We will respond within the timelines required by applicable law (typically 30 days). You may also manage some data directly in the app and revoke Woosh AI's access to Apple or Google sign-in via your Apple ID or Google Account settings.
## 10. Children's Privacy
Woosh AI is not directed to children under 13 (or the minimum age in your jurisdiction, e.g., 16 in parts of the EU). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the applicable minimum age. If you believe a child has provided personal data without proper consent, contact support@cravix.ai and we will delete it promptly.

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:
Know what categories of personal information we collect, use, and disclose (see Sections 1–4)
Request deletion of your personal information, subject to exemptions
Correct inaccurate personal information
Opt out of the sale or sharing of personal information (we do not sell or share your data for cross-context behavioral advertising)

Not be discriminated against for exercising these rights
To exercise these rights, email support@cravix.ai.
